About Us About Us A.M.E.N was founded by a group of entrepreneurs who have managed developed and marketed businesses for over 25 years. These individuals are well versed in the struggles that small businesses investors entrepreneurs and Working Class People experience on a daily basis. They remember the challenges of obtaining the financial support required to gain momentum, the many times they failed before they got it right and the obstacles presented in today's economy. Determined to create a platform to combat each of these issues they pooled their resources to build a network just for you.
